## Project Overview
Sendex is a cutting-edge project aimed at revolutionizing athletic performance tracking for everyone from amateur to professional athletes. Our mission is to develop a smart, connected vest that captures essential performance metrics, making advanced training insights accessible and affordable for all sports clubs.

## Features
The Sendex vest is designed to measure a variety of key performance indicators (KPIs) by collecting real-time data on:
- **Player Location:** Tracks the player's position on the field with precision.
- **Heart Rate (BPM):** Monitors the athlete's heart rate during training sessions and matches.
- **3D Coordinates (XYZ):** Captures the player's movements in three-dimensional space, allowing for detailed analysis.

### Key Metrics and Insights:
- **Maximum Speed:** The fastest speed reached by the player.
- **Average Speed:** The player's speed averaged over the course of a session.
- **Sprint Analysis:** Number of sprints, along with the distance of each sprint.
- **Heatmap Visualization:** A visual representation of the player's movement patterns on the field.
- **And Much More:** Additional metrics designed to provide comprehensive performance insights.

## Vision
The goal of Sendex is to empower small sports clubs by providing them with an affordable, high-quality performance tracking solution. By leveraging the data collected by the Sendex vest, coaches can create customized training programs tailored to each player's strengths and weaknesses, helping athletes reach their full potential.
